Wiki/Biography

Aly Goni was born on Monday, 25 February 1991 (age 29 years; as in 2020) in Bhaderwah, Jammu & Kashmir. His zodiac sign is Pisces.

Aly Goni’s childhood picture

While Aly was in his college, he won the IFTI modelling event which was organised at his college by the Indian Film & Television Institute. In 2012, he participated as a contestant in season five of the youth-based reality show “MTV Splitsvilla.” Aly, then, relocated to Mumbai to pursue his career in acting.

Career

Aly started his acting career in 2013 with the TV serial “Yeh Hai Mohabbatein.” He portrayed the role of ‘Romi Bhalla’ in the show and became a popular face among the audience.

In 2015, he appeared in the role of Raaj Kapoor in the TV serial “Kuch Toh Hai Tere Mere Darmiyaan.” Subsequently, he appeared in serials like “Yeh Kaha Aa Gaye Hum,” “Bahu Hamari Rajni Kant,” “Dhaai Kilo Prem,” and “Naagin 3.”

Kuch Toh Hai Tere Mere Darmiyaan

In 2016, he hosted the TV series “Pyaar Tune Kya Kiya.” Aly participated in the season 9 of the reality show “Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi.” He even appeared as a contestant in the cooking show “Kitchen Champion.” He also plays in the Box Cricket League for the team Chandigarh Cubs.

In 2020, he participated in the game reality show “Bigg Boss 14” as a wild card contestant.
