15 Little Known Facts About Their Friendship

September 2024 Â· 6 minute read

When it comes to the world of television game shows, there are only a select few legendary shows that come to mind. "Wheel Of Fortune" and "Jeopardy!" have taken the lead on modern-day syndicated game shows, and have been doing so for a whopping 56 years for "Jeopardy!", and 45 years for "Wheel Of Fortune".

Both game shows have been immensely successful, mostly due to their hosts, Alex Trebek and Pat Sajak, and have entertained families across the world for decades. If there are two people we would want to be friends, it is both Alex and Pat who have practically devoted their lives to television.

Despite both shows constantly being pitted against one another, and rumours of a rivalry existing for years, Pat Sajak and Alex Trebek have proven what it truly means to be professional. With all that being said, here are 15 little known facts about this dynamic duo's friendship.

15 They Have Been Friends For Years

"Wheel Of Fortune" and 'Jeopardy" have both been on-air for over thirty years each, and have provided decades of entertainment for us viewers. During this time, hosts Alex Trebek and Pat Sajak have been able to form quite a special bond, alongside "Wheel Of Fortune's" legendary Vanna White! These threes friendship is one we can certainly get behind!

14 There Is Never Any Competition

When it comes down to 'Wheel Of Fortune" and "Jeopardy!" it is far too hard to decide which show stands out the most. While some might favour one over the other, Pat and Alex have never let ratings, awards or notoriety get in the way of their friendship. The two have never let the competition between both shows affect any aspect of how they treated one another.

13 They Are Both NBC Royalty

Alex Trebek and Pat Sajak have been on our television screens for the longest time! We have grown up watching these two staple game show hosts entertain us for over 30 years, making them quite the iconic duo. With over three decades in the business, there is no doubt about the fact that these two are definitely NBC royalty.

12 They Support One Another Through Everything

While they may work for the same network, and share the same time slots, Pat Sajak and Alex Trebek are more than just co-workers, they are friends. When it comes down to 'Wheel Of Fortune", and "Jeopardy!", the two hosts have always supported one another and had nothing but great things to say about one another's shows and characters.

11 They Have Won Emmy's Together

Considering both shows have been on the air for quite a long time, it is only fitting for both gameshows to take home a handful of Emmy's throughout its run on air. Both hosts have received a number of Emmy nominations and wins for Outstanding Game Show Host, and rightfully so!

10 They Swapped Jobs Once

Both Alex Trebek and Pat Sajak have a stellar sense of humour, so much that they pulled off one of the best April Fool's jokes in gameshow history. Flashback to 1997 and Alex Trebek hosted 'Wheel Of Fortune", while Pat Sajak participated as a contestant, and in turn, Pat hosted 'Jeopardy!". It definitely got the audience members and fans watching from home very confused!

9 They Never Let The Rumours Get To Them

When it came down to the who's who of CBS gameshows, Alex Trebek and Pat Sajak would often be pinned against one another as to who was the most successful game show host. The media made it seem as if these two were rivals at certain points in their careers, when in fact, they had always shown love for one another and their respective shows.

8 They Have Both Dedicated Their Lives To Their Work

It has been mentioned quite a lot throughout this piece just how much time both "Wheel Of Fortune" and "Jeopardy!" have been on-air. So, if there is one take away from that, it's the fact that bot gameshow hosts have essentially devoted their lives to their respective shows and have proven their loyalty to their viewers.

7 They Share A Lot Of The Same Friends

Considering both Alex Trebek and Pat Sajak have worked for CBS, NBC and ABC for so long, it is no surprise that they have formed friendships with the same people. Throughout their time working with the network, Pat and Alex have gotten exceptionally close to Harry Friedman, the executive producer of both shows, where they both attended his Hollywood Walk of Fame ceremony.

6 They Have Shared Many Milestones Together

Whether it was breaking television records, or collecting countless Emmy's for both 'Wheel Of Fortune', and 'Jeopardy!', Alex Trebek and Pat Sajak have shared a plethora of milestones together, including their most recent at the 2018 NAB Broadcasting Hall of Fame Awards with Vanna White and CBS executive Harry Friedman.

5 Their Families Are Very Close

Considering both Alex Trebek and Pat Sajak have been working alongside one another for the same network, on the same night one game show after the other, it is no surprise that their families have also gotten close. Both the Trebek's and Sajak's are often seen catching up at major events and award shows, and of course, have had a lot of behind the scenes moments.

4 Pat Is Remaining Close To Alex During Health Battle

It was revealed back in March 2019 that Alex Trebek was diagnosed with stage 4 pancreatic cancer. While Trebek is keeping an open mind and has been "responding well to his chemotherapy treatment", fans and friends still remain worries for the host, Pat Sajak included. Pat has made a number of statements regarding the situation, sending his love and support to Alex, claiming how Pat does not know anyone stronger who can fight this off.

3 They Have Both Broken Countless Television Records

Both 'Wheel Of Fortune', and 'Jeopardy!' have been on-air for over 30 years, which is a record in and of itself, however, both shows and hosts have gone on to break countless more records. Both shows have shared the record for the longest-running syndicated show in the United States, and have won countless Emmy's and other awards for their roles on both game shows.

2 They Are Both In It For The Long Run

Pat Sajak and Vanna White have been crushing the 'Wheel Of Fortune' game since 1982, and Alex Trebek with 'Jeopardy' since 1984. They have yet to step today from the position or miss a single day of work then, truly showing that they have been in it for the long run since the very beginning.

1 They Will Both Be Greatly Missed

As the saying goes, all good things come to an end. While Alex Trebek, Pat Sajak and the lovely Vanna White have been fan favourite for over 30 years, there will come a day where they will retire from their positions, and it is safe to say that whenever that day comes, it will certainly be a sad one.
