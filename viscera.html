Biography

Viscera was born in Memphis, TN on February 14, 1972. Viscera, or Big Daddy V, was a WWF wrestler who held the Hardcore and World Tag Team titles. He was billed at 6’9″ and 487 pounds. As part of the WWF’s Ministry of Darkness, he attacked his leader, The Undertaker, and feuded with Kane, the leader’s brother. He began his pro wrestling career as one half the Harlem Knights alongside storyline brother Bobby. They became Mabel and Mo when they transitioned to the WWF.
