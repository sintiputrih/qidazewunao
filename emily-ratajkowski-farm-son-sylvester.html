Emily Ratajkowski Spends Quality Time on a Farm with Son Sly

February 2024 · 2 minute read

Emily Ratajkowski is taking a breather from her fast-paced life and spending quality time outdoors with her loved ones.

Yesterday, the newly single mom of one shared photos to her Instagram Story featuring the model enjoying a sunny day on a farm with her son, Sylvester Apollo Bear.

Emily Ratajkowski/Instagram

The photos included a sweet picture of baby Sly being introduced to some donkeys and a shot of EmRata embracing the cowboy aesthetic—posing in a car in a lavender halter top, loose high-waisted jeans, and a pair of black cowboy boots.

Emily Ratajkowski/Instagram

The My Body author also posted photos from the nature-filled getaway on Monday. In an Instagram carousel, she included snaps of her holding her baby boy in a pasture surrounded by trees, as well as one of her playing on the swings with him and another of her and her girlfriend's shadows on the grass.

For the lovely outing, Ratajkowski dressed casually in a cropped white T-shirt and black wide-leg yoga pants.

She simply captioned the post with an emoji of two hands making a heart.

Ratajkowski's peaceful getaway with her son comes after she reportedly split from her husband of four years, Sebastian Bear-McClard.

Though the model has not publicly addressed the breakup, she seemed to confirm the news last month by liking a string of tweets relating to the split and the film producer's alleged infidelity.

Ratajkowski and Bear-McClard married in 2018 in a low-key ceremony at a courthouse attended only by a handful of their closest friends. Three years later, in March of 2021, the pair welcomed their son.
