WWE Star Montez Ford Is Happily Married To Wife Bianca Belair With Two Kids

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Montez Ford is a 32-year-old pro wrestler from Chicago, Illinois. Recently signing with World Wrestling Entertainment, he appeared on the Raw brand.

Together with Angelo Dawkins, he makes up one-half of the tag team known as the Street Profits.

On 26 September 2015, he made his debut in the house show as part of a 20-Man Battle Royal to choose the top contender for the WWE NXT Title.

In the October 26 house show, the professional wrestler prevailed in a fatal four-way match against members of competing tag teams Nick Miller of  the team The Mighty, Rowe of team War Raiders, and Tucker Knight of team Heavy Machinery.

Who Is Montez Ford? Wikipedia Bio of WWE Star?

Chicago native Montez Ford is a former EVOLVE Tag Team Champion alongside Dawkins. Also, he holds the SmackDown Tag Team champions title.

His birth name is Kenneth Crawford. Formerly, he was in the USA Marine Corps between September 2008 to September 2012.

Ford is currently 32-year-old born to his parents in 1990 in the United States. He grew up in North Carolina, where he completed his high school.

He developed into an outstanding track athlete while a student at Anson High School, where he won the 2007 Athlete of the Year award.

In his early days, he also participated in track and field as well as the Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps (JROTC).

Later, Ford decided to enlist in the US Marine Corps instead of going to college by his senior year out of financial obligation to his mother, who was going through a divorce.

The 6-foot-1-inch athlete was initially interested in the professional wrestling business; however, he has no idea how to get started.

His wrestling career was not an overnight achievement. Thanks to his wife who uploaded his pictures on the WWE recruitment page. Luckily, he received a call four months later inviting him to attend a WWE tryout.

Montez Ford Is Father Of Two Kids With Wife Bianca Belair- His Family

Montez Ford tied the wedding knot with his wife, Bianca Belair, on 23 June 2018. The couple just had their fourth wedding anniversary in 2022.

Ford and Belair connected at the WWE Performance Center, and the two became engaged in 2017.

After getting married in June 2018, the couple graduated from NXT. Now, they are parents to two children i.e. a daughter and a son.

Ford regularly posts pictures of these kids on his social handle. Recently, he wrote a special message for his children on Instagram.

It read, “I thank God for you. Watching you grow is the best & saddest thing at the same time, but I’m grateful for every moment of it.”

The husband-wife duo is presently essential component of WWE programming.

As a member of The Street Proftis alongside Angelo Dawkins, Bianca is currently in the midst of her second big world title reign. Ford has held the title of tag team champion on Raw, SmackDown, and NXT.
