Sydney Brooke Simpson went through A LOT of negative experiences growing up. Fans may know Sydney as OJ Simpson and Nicole Brown Simpson’s daughter, but there's a lot more to her. It's been more than two decades since people last heard from Sydney and now that she's all grown up, most of us are curious to find out what’s going on with her life.

Sydney was born on October 17th, 1985, and she has a younger brother, Justin Ryan Simpson who was born in 1988. Before their famous father, OJ Simpson, became an NFL football player, and met and married their mother Nicole Brown, OJ was previously married to his first wife, Marguerite Simpson. In OJ's first marriage, they had three children together; Arnelle Simpson, Jason Simpson, and Aaren Simpson. Sadly, one of OJ's children passed away at only 2 years old in 1979.

OJ Simpson and Nicole Brown met in a nightclub where Nicole was working as a waitress. When they met, OJ was still married to his first wife Marguerite Simpson, but later divorce took place due to cheating allegations and extramarital affairs. OJ moved on with his life and married Nicole Brown, but their union ended up in divorce as well, filed by Nicole on the claims of irreconcilable differences.

Updated April 2024: On April 11th, 2024, it was reported that OJ Simpson died at the age of 76 after a long battle with Cancer. His family posted the news on social media.

How Was Life For Sydney Brooke Simpson Growing Up?

Sydney grew up in the limelight due to her association with her famous father OJ Simpson, who was recognized nationwide as being a famous football player and actor. Life for Sydney was okay before tragedy hit their family. However, not much is known about what she loved to do as a child or what her favorite games were.

As little children, Sydney and her younger brother Jason were close, and being that she was the older one, she always looked out for her brother.

Sydney chose to live in privacy as she was growing older, and with that, she even took the measures of changing her name so that she could live in disguise and peace from the public.

This Is What OJ Simpson Was Accused Of Doing

OJ Simpson was accused of being the main suspect in the demise of his ex-wife Nicole Brown Simpson and her friend Ronald Goldman, when they were found by the police outside Nicole's townhouse in Brentwood, California on June 13th, 1994.

The retired football star claimed he was innocent, and so he hired highly influential 'Dream Team' lawyers as they were widely known to defend him in court. By the way, Robert Kardashian was one of OJ's Lawyers.

After a public 11-month trial, the jury found OJ not guilty, and the trial was brought to a close. Many people who were watching from across the nation thought that there was no justice served to the family of Nicole Brown.

OJ Simpson carried on with life and even did interviews with various TV stations. OJ also wrote a book in connection with his innocence, but some people were not buying it.

If it wasn't already obvious that OJ committed the m*****, this confession should remove all doubts. The only way to come away with the alternate conclusion is if you really, really, wanted to believe it."

Another one went on to say, "I mean, just look at the cover of the book. Does anyone think it's an accident that 'I DID IT' is printed in highly visible red font whereas 'IF' is printed in a white color that blends into his jacket until you look closely. It's a pretty brilliant cover design, actually."

Fairly enough, during and after the trial, Sydney and her brother Justin were kept far away from the spotlight.

What Is Sydney Brooke Simpson Doing With Her Life Today?

As we were all expecting and hoping, Sydney Brooke Simpson has achieved quite a lot for herself throughout the years. If we back it up a little bit, after graduation, the now 38-year-old took up work as an events coordinator in Atlanta. She then ventured into the real estate business, which prompted her to relocate to St. Petersburg, Florida. Sydney started her own business there and named it Simpsy Properties LLC. She's thought to own a restaurant as well.

Sydney's brother Justin is in the real estate business too.

With all these businesses in mind, Sydney Brooke Simpson has managed to accumulate a net worth of $500,000, according to Skinny Scoop.

Sydney is not in a known relationship at the moment. Let's just say, if she is, she's kept it under wraps (although in the past, according to Celeb Suburb, Sydney was rumored to have dated the then St. Petersburg City Council candidate Robert Blackmon, who later publicly denied the rumors).

Also, Sydney has done so much for herself when it comes to her body that she decided to lose weight and become leaner, healthier, and stronger. According to Beauty Positive, OJ's daughter has lost 50 pounds from the 200 pounds she used to weigh.

Although her brother can be found on social media, Sydney decided to keep on living a private life, so at the moment, she does not have a social media account.

Sydney is believed to have purchased a home in Tampa Bay, Florida, and lives there, enjoying her life every waking day.
