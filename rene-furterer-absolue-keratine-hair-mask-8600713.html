Shoppers Say Their Thin Hair Feels Stronger Thanks to This Mask

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Heat styling is oftentimes damaging to hair. I’ve been flat ironing my hair for years, and unfortunately, it shows. I’ve battled dryness and breakage on a daily basis, and it’s not just heat that can do real damage to hair; chemical treatments, color, and tight hairstyles can also make your strands become extremely fragile. Luckily, there are a handful of nourishing shampoos, serums, masks, and more that can give TLC to your hair. When it comes to the next one on my list, it’s Rene Furterer’s Absolue Kératine Ultimate Repairing Mask.  

The formula is specifically made to revive damaged, over-processed hair. It contains a plant-based keratin to repair strands, a camelina oil filled with fatty acids to moisturize and protect, and a biocymentine extract – which is rich in ceramides — to help retain moisture for stronger hair, per the brand. Additionally, the Absolue Kératine Ultimate Repairing Mask promises to leave your tresses detangled, soft, and shiny. And thanks to its lightweight texture, it won’t weigh your strands down. Keep in mind that this product is made for those with fine to medium hair. This intensive treatment should be used for four to six weeks post-shampoo for only two to three times a day. 

Reviewers vouch that this Rene Furterer mask has saved their weakened hair. “It made my hair lustrous and shiny again! It no longer felt like straw,” said a reviewer. A second shopper shared that their thinning, color-treated hair has “regained strength and body.” Even those with dry hair are fans of this strengthening formula. A customer said their bleached-blonde hair is now “soft, smooth, and shiny,” while another agrees that it makes their dehydrated hair feel “smooth and silky” in hot, Southwest summers.
