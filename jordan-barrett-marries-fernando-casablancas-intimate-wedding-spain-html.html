Jordan Barrett marries Fernando Casablancas at an intimate wedding in Spain

January 2024 · 4 minute read

When Jordan Barrett announced his engagement last month, most people assumed that he'd found himself a lucky lady.

But on Wednesday, the Australian model, 24, shocked fans by revealing he is planning on marrying fellow male model Fernando Casablancas, 23, following an intimate ceremony in Ibiza, Spain.

The ceremony was attended by no more than 15 of Jordan's closet friends including models Kate Moss, Georgia May Jagger and American playwright Jeremy O. Harris.
