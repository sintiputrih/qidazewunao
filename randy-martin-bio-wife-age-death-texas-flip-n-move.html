Randy Martin Bio, Wife, Age, Death

March 2024 · 5 minute read

Texas Flip N Move star Randy Martin sadly died at the age of 65. His passing was announced on Facebook via a post on 17 August 2022. According to the reports of Deadline, he died at his home from liver cancer. TMZ had previously reported that he was diagnosed with cancer in May 2022.

The Facebook announcement says, “We are broken-hearted to let you know that Randy passed away early this morning. Please pray for his wife and family as they navigate this difficult time. You will be greatly missed.”

The article includes a video that is jam-packed with all of Randy’s priceless recollections. The majority of the images in the slideshow reflect how vivacious and joyful Randy actually was in real life. He and his wife Judy can be seen in one of the pictures on their wedding day many years ago.

Texas Flip N Move Star Randy Martin Death Details

In a separate post, Texas Flip N Move star Randy Martin’s brother Robert wrote a heartbreaking post, “My brother, Granville Martin aka Randy Martin, passed away this morning @6:15am. Randy lost his battle with cancer, after being diagnosed in May-2022. Randy had just lost his son, Mark Martin, 44 years old, last month to cancer. As a family, we have lost, Dad, Ronnie Martin, 4 years ago, Mark Martin, 1 month ago, and Randy Martin, today, all 3, lost to cancer.”

Furthermore, he added, “We are devastated as a family and they will all be dearly missed. I will try to make another post, pertaining to the services for Granville “Randy” Martin, in the next day or so. Randy was loved dearly by his family, friends, and some acquaintances. Randy was very giving and very helpful to everyone he met, this made him very easy to love.”

Robert concluded, “I love you more than I can express in words, as they do you no justice. You had a very giving and helping heart brother. Save me a place, as I will be there shortly brother, RIP my brother, you are and were, the absolute best brother I could have ever asked for. I will see you soon, may God bless you and yours my brother, you have been my HERO. Much LOVE until we meet again. Your loving little brother.”

Fans are saddened by his death and have left comments on the post. One fan wrote, “He will be greatly missed by so many. We loved watching his talent, skills, laughter & loving personality come to life on Texas Flip N Move. Our family’s thoughts & prayers are with his family”.

Likewise, another fan wrote, “So sorry for your loss! We loved watching him. May God give you comfort!”.

Fans had loved him on the show and would call him “Lone Wolf”.
