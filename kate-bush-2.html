Kate Bush also known as Catherine Bush (CBE) is an English music artist, singer, record producer, songwriter, dancer, pianist and musician. As of 2023, Kate Bush’s net worth is $70 million. She accumulated her net worth through her singing career.

She mainly gained massive media attention with she released her debut single “Wuthering Heights” after being signed by EMI. The single went up to the top spot on the UK singles chart. This created a pioneering feat for her as she became the first female artist to reach the number 1 spot which was also written by herself.

After getting discovered at the age of 11 by Pink Floyd guitarist, David Gilmour, Bush quickly got a major deal with EMI records. Kate has a record of creating top-level albums such as The Kick Inside, House of Love, Director’s Cut, etc. Notably, all of them reached UK Top 10 Album chart.

Kate since has released numerous hit Top 40 singles including “The Man with the Child in His Eyes”, “Running Up That Hill”, “Don’t Give Up”, “Babooshka”, and “King of the Mountain” etc. Kate earned the feat of becoming the top female artist in the UK to top the album chart at no. 1 position.
