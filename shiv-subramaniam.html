Shiv Subramaniam’s Age & Early Life

Shiv Subramaniam was born on December 23, 1959, in Bombay. He spent his entire existence in Bombay. Shiv stayed a Shree Shivaji Preparatory Military School pupil in Pune, where he graduated from secondary school in 1976, according to his LinkedIn profile. Shiv Subramaniam stayed a Science, History, and Languages student at his institution. During his school years, he was involved in recreational activities such as acting, school newspaper writing, horseback riding, football, basketball, and many theater performances. Then in 1977, he proceeded to Saint Xavier’s College where he studied B.A. in English Literature and also performed drama at college.

How much is Shiv Subramaniam’s net worth? Shiv has amassed a net worth of 12 Crore by acting in high-profile films as a performer and director. Career

Shiv began his performing career in 1989 and has since performed in a variety of films, as we previously stated. He is best known for creating the character of businessman I.M Virani for the Colors TV network’s Mukti Bandhan in 2011. His main parts in the industry include his portrayal of Ananya’s father in the film 2 States, Mr. Bose in Adolescent Patti, Peter in That Girl in Yellow Footwear, and Head Of School in Hichki.

Aside from this, we have mentioned a few more films in which he worked and received a lot of praise. Shiv Subramaniam received the Filmfare Award for Best Screenplay in 2006 for the film Hazaaron Khwaishein Aisi. He was also nominated for a Filmfare Award for best script for his maiden film Parinda, on which he also served as an associate director.
