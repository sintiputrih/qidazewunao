Lauren Bushnell's 'Bachelor' Finale Dress: The Look for Less

February 2024 · 2 minute read

We’re giving out two more roses — to the Bachelor ladies’ finale dresses!

Lauren Bushnell and JoJo Fletcher both stunned in their evening gowns on The Bachelor‘s season finale on Monday, March 14, and we have the details on both contestants’ styles.

PHOTOS: 10 Times The Bachelor Made Our Hearts Explode

Bushnell, who scored the final rose and an engagement proposal from Ben Higgins, wore the fitted cobalt Badgley Mischka Odess Halter Gown. She teamed the design with long dangling earrings by Suzanne Kalan — and, soon enough, a 3-carat Neil Lane diamond engagement ring with an emerald-cut center stone, which is worth a cool $100,000.

Runner-up Fletcher chose a baby-pink Mac Duggal gown with a plunging neckline and sequin embellishments. The real estate developer did not receive the final rose, but she did land a new title: Fletcher will be the new Bachelorette!
