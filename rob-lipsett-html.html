Rob Lipsett - Age, Family, Bio

Irish fitness trainer whose YouTube and Instagram stardom helped propel his Lipsett Fitness brand. He has more than 600,000 Instagram followers to go along with 470,000 YouTube subscribers. In 2018, he began appearing on the TV dating show Love Island. He has been sponsored by Alphalete fitness products.

Before Fame

He decided to forego college and pursue a career as a fitness trainer, starting out on Facebook before joining Instagram and YouTube in 2014.

Trivia

He played rugby at a high level while in secondary school, but he found himself more in love with the training than the actual sport.

Family Life

He started dating fellow fitness Instagram model Sarah Godfrey in 2011. They would later split. He's since been romantically linked to Linda Smyth. He has three older sisters.

Associated With

He posted a vlog in 2016 where he went to Gold's Gym in Venice, California and worked out with Calum Von Moger and Steve Cook.
