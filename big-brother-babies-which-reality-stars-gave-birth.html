Big Brother Babies! Which Reality Stars Gave Birth

June 2024 · 1 minute read

Many former Big Brother stars started families after leaving the CBS show.

Jessica Graf and Cody Nickson, who sparked a showmance in season 19, announced in September 2018 that they were expecting their first child together. (Nickson welcomed his daughter Paisley with a previous partner in 2012.)

Graf gave birth to their first daughter, Maverick, in March 2019. The couple have since welcomed three more daughters together: Carter in October 2020, Atlas in July 2022 and Gemma in December 2023.

Nickson and Graf aren’t the only Big Brother contestants to welcome children following their reality TV stints. Bayleigh Dayton and Chris “Swaggy C” Williams, who met in the Big Brother house during season 20, welcomed their first baby, daughter Alora, in August 2022. Nearly one year later, Dayton announced her second pregnancy.
