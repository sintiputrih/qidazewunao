Morgan Wallen fans are furious at the cost of presale tickets to his 2024 tour

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Morgan Wallen added a couple of more dates for his One Night At A Time tour but people are not happy as fans are furious to see the cost of presale tickets for the 2024 event.

Fans have been complaining about the high costs of the tickets ever since Ticketmaster faced backlash for the cost of Taylor Swift tickets. Now, Morgan Wallen is facing heat from the fans as many complained about the high cost of tickets for his upcoming 2024 One Night At A Time tour.

Morgan Fallen fans slam ticket prices for 2024 tour

Morgan extended his 2023 tour by adding a couple of dates in 2024. While fans were excited, their happiness quickly turned into disappointment when they noticed the price for the upcoming shows. Some pointed out that the lowest ticket price they could find was $400 while another pointed out that party pit seats were $599 plus fees. It did not take long for fans to slam the cost.

One wrote on Twitter: “Concerts have become an “elite only” event for top acts and that’s a shame.. keep in mind, these were face value for a Thursday night concert in Indianapolis and BEFORE you added about 20% on top for Ticketmaster/servicing fees. No, thank you, Morgan Wallen…” Another added: “Just got off the Morgan Wallen waitlist for tickets at US Bank Stadium next summer and the cheapest ticket was 300 before fees. Gyat DAMN I love MW as much as the next but that’s insanity.”

“Was SO excited to get access to presale to buy tickets, but are you kidding?!?! @MorganWallen I’ll always be a fan but I will have to support you from home,” wrote one more. “#TBT to when I met@MorganWallen for $150. Now you can’t even get nosebleed tickets to his shows for that price. Yikes. $900 for PIT? I was 2nd row PIT for $450 just last year. This shit is insane. Sorry not sorry but the average American can’t afford to see ya anymore MW,” said another.
