Choupo-Moting biography.

Jean-Eric Maxim Choupo-Moting is a professional Cameroonian footballer who plays for Bayern Munich and the Cameroon National Team as a forward.

The German-born Cameroonian footballer started his professional career in 2006-2007 when he joined Hamsburger SV. He spent 5 years at the club. His first season was spent at the academy side and his remaining 4 seasons at the main side.

He made a total of 31 appearances with a goal to his name for Hamsburger SV II, and registered 37 appearances with 5 goals to his name for the Hamsburger SV Bundesliga side.

Choupo then joined Mainz 05 in 2011-2012 in the German Bundesliga. He stayed there for 3 seasons and made a total of 81 appearances with 22 goals to his name before his departure from the club.

He then joined Schalke 04 in 2014-2015 and had a 3-year stay at the Bundesliga side. He made a total of 106 appearances with 22 goals to his name for the club.

Choupo-Moting then changed leagues and joined Stoke City for a year in the English Premier League during the 2017-2018 season. He made a total of 32 appearances with 5 goals to his name for the club.

He later joined Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) in 2018-2019 in the French Ligue 1. He stayed there for 2 years and made a total of 51 appearances with 9 goals to his name for the French club.

Choupo-Moting is currently having an amazing time at Bayern Munich after joining the club in 2020-2021. He has been with the Bundesliga club for 3 years and made a total of 86 appearances with 35 goals to his name for the club so far in all competitions.
