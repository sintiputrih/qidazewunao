Career

Mohammed started his professional career with the Kumasi Barcelona Babies F.C. team and then joined the African Talent Football Academy in the southern Ghanaian town of Nsawam. He agreed to terms with Southampton on a four-year professional deal for £10.9 million. On September 21, 2021, he scored his first goal in the EFL Cup against Sheffield United.
