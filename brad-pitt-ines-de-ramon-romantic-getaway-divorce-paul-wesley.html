Brad Pitt And Ines De Ramon Spotted On A Romantic Getaway

Brad Pitt and Ines De Ramon are seemingly getting more and more serious if their latest romantic getaway is any indication. The actor and his girlfriend were spotted walking along the beach in Santa Barbara with their arms around each other. They were also seen walking their dogs, in an undeniable domestic display.

Brad Pitt is really happy with Ines De Ramon, as this is his first serious relationship since his divorce from Angelina Jolie. The couple have reportedly been living together for a couple of months now, and "he couldn't be happier" with their new living situation.

"They were spending so much time together at Brad’s that it just made sense for Ines to move in with him," a source told PEOPLE. "Brad’s very happy and loves spending time with her. Moving in together was a natural thing."

"Since the divorce, Brad’s dated but never seriously," the insider went on. "This is the first serious relationship, and he couldn’t be happier." The source described Ines as "kind, funny and very special", adding that it was "amazing" to see the actor happy in a relationship again.

"With Ines, he found his spark again," they stated. "It’s truly amazing to see. Brad’s a great guy. He deserves to be happy."

Ines De Ramon And Paul Wesley Recently Finalized Their Divorce

Ines De Ramon and The Vampire Diaries actor Paul Wesley finalized their divorce only a little under two months ago. The pair separated for five months before the actor filed for divorce back in February last year. The separation and consequent divorce was reportedly mutual and amicable, and they have kept the details of their divorce private.

It allegedly went pretty smoothly, and Paul Wesley's filing was "uncontested." Both "parties have entered into a written agreement regarding their property and their marriage or domestic partnership rights, including support, the original of which is being or has been submitted to the court," the filing read.

It was just days after Ines De Ramon's divorce was finalized that sources alleged Brad Pitt was pushing for his own lengthy divorce to reach a resolution, even if that included giving up his request for custody of his and Angelina Jolie's children.
