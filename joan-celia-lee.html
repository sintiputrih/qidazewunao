Joan Celia Lee is an actress, writer, and producer. Joan Celia Lee is best known for being the daughter of the legendary comic book writer, publisher, and producer, Stan Lee.

Early Life and Childhood

Caption: Joan Celia Lee with her father Stan Lee (Source: TMZ)

Joan Celia Lee was born in April 1950 in New York City, United States of America. Her present age is 73 years old. Moreover, she is an American citizen. And She belongs to the white ethnicity. She is the daughter of Joan Boocock Lee and Stan Lee. Her mother was a British-American model and voice actress. Her father Stan Lee is a legendary comic book writer, publisher, and producer. Talking about her siblings, she has a younger sister, Jan Lee, who died only three days after her birth.

She was raised in a very creative environment and learned a lot about the artistic aspects of comic book making quite early in her life. As the daughter of a comic book writer, she learned to make comic book layouts while also taking an active interest in media, as her parents were part of the industry. At an early age, she was interested in painting, craftwork, fashion design, and cosmetics making. As an adult age, she went on to gain experience at ‘Marvel Studios.’

Sadly, she lost her mother to a fatal stroke a year before her father Stan Lee passed away. Legendary comic book author and writer died on 12 November 2018. Stan Lee was suffering from vision problems and pneumonia for a long time causing his death.
