Emmanuelle Vaugier Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Emmanuelle Vaugier (born June 23, 1976) is a Canadian actress, singer, and model who has worked in cinema and television. On CSI: NY, she played Detective Jessica Angell, Mia on Two and a Half Men, Dr. Helen Bryce on Smallville, FBI Special Agent Emma Barnes on Human Target, and The Morrigan on Lost Girl, among other roles. In the feature film Secondhand Lions, Vaugier co-starred with Michael Caine and Robert Duvall. She played Addison Corday in Saw II and Saw IV, and she also featured in the Josh Hartnett film 40 Days and 40 Nights as a supporting character.
