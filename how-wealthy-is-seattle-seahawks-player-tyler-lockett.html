How Wealthy Is Seattle Seahawks Player Tyler Lockett

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Seattle Seahawks think wide receiver Tyler Lockett is a smart investment. They are giving this gifted speedster the deal he merits as a result.

Tyler Lockett has amassed an estimated net worth of $29.0 million from his NFL career alone as a result of the value he adds to the Seahawks’ game. And over the next few years, his contract will only increase his wealth.

What is the Net Worth of Tyler Lockett? Salary, Earnings

Tyler Lockett had a total salary of almost $1.4 million when he joined with the NFL in 2015. Included in there were a salary of $435,000 and a signing bonus of $786,751.

He received salaries of $580,000 and $695,000 for the two seasons after that. But as each season went on, the Seahawks saw the value of retaining Lockett on their roster and extended his deal.

According to the terms of his current contract, Lockett’s signing bonus and $2.0 million salary totaled $11.0 million in 2018. His 2020 salary will increase to $10.0 million from his 2019 earnings of $5.5 million.

Over the course of six seasons, Lockett earned $29.19 million in his whole career. In 2021, he’s anticipated to earn another $10.0 million.

The Contracts of Tyler Lockett

Kevin Lockett, a former NFL wide receiver, gave birth to Tyler Lockett on September 28, 1992. His ability to excel on the football field is genetic.

The native of Oklahoma was a standout student at Tulsa’s Booker T. Washington High School. His father’s alma university, Kansas State University, awarded him an athletic scholarship due to his abilities.

He was selected by the Seattle Seahawks in the 2015 NFL Draft thanks to his outstanding collegiate career. His rookie contract’s specifics aren’t known.

The Seahawks recognized a bankable athlete in Lockett as he developed from a rookie to a guy with incredible professional stats. They thus agreed to a three-year contract extension with him in August 2018.

The enormous $31.8 million contract would keep him with the team through 2022. The contract included a signing bonus of $9 million. He has a guaranteed $14.9 million and a rising salary through 2022.

Inn of Tyler Lockett

Perhaps the motivation Lockett needed to establish a base close to the Seahawks’ stadium came from a hard-earned deal. He spent a significant amount of money on a home in 2019.

For $1.97 million, Lockett acquired a brand-new building in Sammamish, Washington. The home has five bedrooms and 4.75 bathrooms, and it is 4,572 square feet in size.
