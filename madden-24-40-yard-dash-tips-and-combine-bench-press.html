Madden 24 40 Yard Timing

Ideal timing for the 40-yard dash can be set in the range of 4.6 seconds to 4.9 seconds at the fastest. On average, it can go up from 4.23 to 4.26. Generally, seconds below five are considered good.

For example, if you picked a player with a dash run time of 4.24 seconds, it is in the speed range of 97, 98, and 99. This indicates your player will have at least 97 speed which can go up to 98 or 99.

Madden 24 Combine Bench Press

Madden 24 offers you to go through NFL Combine Bench Press drills where you can achieve maximum reps to improve your rating.

It is an important part of NFL Scouting that will highly determine the player’s strength. The average number varies according to the position. 

How To Do Bench Press? 

In Madden 24, once you are inside NFL Combine completing all the previous activities up to determining your avatar’s physique, you will be led to the scouting event. There your first drill will be the bench press. To perform that:

  • On the screen, you will see a bar with green and red colors. The upper red indicates your stamina penalty. The lower green is your lift target and the upper green is your release target.
  • Push both LS and RS buttons together and release in the target zone. 
  • When the bar falls, you need to build up your stamina by pressing LB and RB rapidly, often called bumpers.
  • You will have to repeat the same steps as long as you can to reach the maximum reps. 

The ideal value for a bench press with a 99-strength score is 45.7. This also depends upon the position you have chosen to go with. The CB and LB positions will have different reps averages.

For example, a player achieves 32 reps at a combine and 35 on the Pro Day. Now, if we look at the chart above, both numbers should fit into one single strength. The 87 and 88 Strength fits the 31-35 reps and 32-36 reps range in them respectively. 

This means the player’s overall strength must be between 87 and 88. In this way, you can determine the strength of your player as well as the bench press score.

How To Play Superstar Mode Madden 24?

Madden 24 Superstar mode is a career mode to take you on the journey of stardom beginning from Rookie, entering the NFL Draft, playing leagues, and more. 

The main features include NFL Combine which will lead you through various mini-games and drills to improve your player’s ratings prior to the draft. It also has an in-game Player Grading System exclusively available on the mode to give you real-time feedback on the performances after they start playing by signing their contract. 

It also comes with Superstar missions that let you go behind your focused and comprehensive tasks and bring you rewards. The social media reactions will center around you and the mode will have a number of other side activities.

In the Superstar Showdown, you can compete with other superstars all over the world in a fast-paced 3 vs. 3 football mode. The progression and Customization feature will let you create multiple avatars in multiple positions and determine your progression path.

To play the Superstar Mode, You have to:

  • Once you are inside Superstar Mode, you have to create or start a new superstar career.
  • Choose any of your desired position to start your career in the role.
  • Then you will get to create your profile by filling in the personal details.
  • Click ‘Continue’.
  • Customize your appearances.
  • Adjust and modify your body’s physique and archetype.
  • After that, you will reach the ‘Path to NFL’ interface in the ‘Home’ tab. There you get to set on a journey starting from NFL Combine. 
  • After you have completed the drills in NFL Combine, You will have to face Combine Interview and try to answer all questions correctly. 
  • After that, you will get to NFL Draft where you will be picked by a team and sign you in. 
  • This way, your regular NFL Journey begins.
