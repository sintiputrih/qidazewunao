Beside this, does SNA have a curfew?The curfew prohibits departures from 10 p.m. to 7 a.m. every day except Sundays, when it runs from 10 p.m. to 8 a.m. Arrivals are prohibited between 11 p.m. and 7 a.m. Mondays-Saturdays and until 8 a.m. Sundays.Also, where can I park overnight at John Wayne Airport? Parking Information Curbside Valet Parking – $10 per hour up to $30 per 24-hour period. Terminal Parking (A1, A2, B2, C) – $2 per hour up to $20 per 24-hour period. Cell Phone Waiting Lot – Free. Main Street (Off-Airport) Parking – $2 per hour up to $14 per 24-hour period. Besides, what time does SNA open? The airport is open from 4:30AM until 12:00AM.Where do you pick up passengers at SNA Airport?Lyft, Uber and Wingz are allowed to pick-up their passengers on the top level of parking structures A2 and B2, and in the Terminal C parking structure on the upper level next to the ticketing area. Ride app drivers are allowed to drop off passengers on the Departures (upper) Level at curbside.
