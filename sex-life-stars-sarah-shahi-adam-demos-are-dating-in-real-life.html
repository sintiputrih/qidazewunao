'Sex/Life' Stars Sarah Shahi &amp; Adam Demos Are Dating in Real Life!

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Fans who watch the new Netflix series Sex/Life are going to love this… Sarah Shahi and Adam Demos are dating in real life!

Sarah, 41, plays a woman in a love triangle with her husband (Mike Vogel) and her ex-boyfriend (Demos), who she can’t get out of her heard.

Throughout the series, we watch Sarah‘s character look back at her former relationship and the passionate exploits she had with her ex. Eventually, he comes back into her life and causes trouble in her marriage.

Sarah and Adam‘s chemistry is evident on screen and it looks like it continued off screen.

Click inside for more details on the couple’s relationship…

The couple confirmed their relationship on Instagram back on December 31, 2020 and they’re still going strong today.

In an interview with Entertainment Tonight, Sarah joked about falling in love with Adam on set. She said, “It was great casting. I’ll put it that way!” He added, “Yeah, they didn’t get it wrong!”

Adam‘s birthday was on May 22 and Sarah wrote the sweetest tribute for him. She said, “Not exactly sure how two people on opposite sides of the world could have more in common, were meant to meet, were meant to be together. But I do know I met my soulmate. I do know I found my forever. I do know I’ve never loved deeper, harder, more ferociously. I do know I’m overly grateful for him. I do know I’ve loved him for a thousand lifetimes before and will love him for a thousand lifetimes more. Happy birthday my baby ❤️.”

Adam commented on the birthday post and said, “My forever and more.. I love you so fucking much my baby ❤️❤️❤️❤️.”

Everyone is talking about that full frontal scene from Adam in episode three!
