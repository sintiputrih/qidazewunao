All songs in Fast X

July 2024 · 3 minute read

The Fast and the Furious franchise has become known for a lot of things as it has evolved over the years. From its humble beginnings as a street racing and crime drama to the bombastic spectacle we’ve come to expect of it in recent years, the series has been elevated to a status of both reverence, and a complete and utter meme. Family, anyone? 

Recommended Videos

Nevertheless – one thing that has managed to stay somewhat consistent over the course of the saga is its soundtrack, with a wide variety of hip hop, rap, and R&B bangers accompanying scene transitions, more often than not which involve wide, sweeping shots which speed up and slow down to the rhythm of the beat, all the while showcase beautiful cars, vistas, and people.

Undoubtedly, the imminent tenth entry, Fast X will be no different – and ahead of its release, Universal has released the full tracklist for the upcoming action flick and posted it all over social media. So, if you’re looking to build up your hype playlist ahead of the film’s release – or you’re walking out of the theater with a song stuck in your head, without further ado – here’s every track that features in Fast X. 

‘Fast X’ tracklist 

The above post on the official “Fast Saga” Instagram page shows off each and every track that will feature in what is sure to be the most ludicrous (pun not intended) action movies of the year, but in case you’re after an itemized list you can copy and paste into your music streaming service of choice, below is a list of every song in the film:

  • The End of the Road Begins (Intro) – Kai Cenat
  • Datura – $uicide Boys
  • Spinnin – Lil Durk (feat. Est Gee)
  • Furious – BIA
  • Get It – Anti Da Menace, Luh Tyler
  • Toretto – J Balvin
  • Won’t Back Down – Youngboy Never Broke Again, Dermot Kennedy & Bailey Zimmerman
  • Sigue La Fiesta – Justin Quiles, Dalex & Santa Fe Klan
  • Angel Pt. 1 – Kodak Black & Nle Choppa (feat. Jimin of BTS, Jvke, & Muni Long)
  • Gasolina (Safari Riot Remix) – Daddy Yankee (feat Myke Towers)
  • My City – 24KGoldn, Kane Brown & G Herbo
  • Vai Sentando – Skrillex (feat Ludmilla, King Doudou & Duki)
  • Countin’ On You – Lil Tjay, Friday & Khi Infinite
  • Bando (Fast X Remix) – Anna (feeat, Madman & Gemitaiz)
  • Reaper – Babyface Ray, Babytron & Peezy
  • Let’s Ride – YG & The Notorious B.I.G. (feat Lambo4oe, TY Dolla $ign & Bone Thugs-N-Harmony)
  • 9 In My Hand (Fast X Remix) Kordhell & Key Glock
  • Nothing Else Matters – Jessie Murph

So there you have it – a nice blend of old and new, the extensive list of artists and tracks that will feature in the upcoming film is sure to boom nice and loud as all of the action of the Toretto family’s next adventure unfolds.

Fast X speeds its way into theaters on May 19, 2023.
